F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do you decide who to match me with?

    Our matching isn't only based on your goals (although that's an important part of it). We consider lots of "significant" factors, such as experience with that goal, age range, how fast you want to complete it. We also consider some less significant ones - what country are you based in? what are your hobbies? Did you have breakfast this morning?

  • What sort of goals can I pursue?

    Anything and everything. People pursue goals for all sorts of reasons - work, love, intrigue. We don't want to limit that. Once you create your profile we'll ask you to rank all the goals you have and then let you know the appropriate accountability team. So Artic Pottery enthusiasts, we're here for you!

  • Is the platform currently operational?

    We're operational and are currently in the early-access phase at the moment, and you're one of the lucky individuals who've stumbled upon us. So if you'd like early-entry and a chance to contribute your thoughts as we build, sign up.

  • How does the pandemic affect meeting my team?

    It doesn't. We match you remotely. Work from anywhere. On a schedule that suits you all.

  • Is this free?

    Matchmaking based on dreams will always be free. We would have additional features in the future that will help support us running this financially.
